THE BATMAN BEGINS...

In Matt Reeves's new detective spin on the classic hero, Robert Pattinson is donning the cowl as the caped crusader, Batman.
Gotham has become ruthless world where corruption and crime plagues the streets and a mysterious killer is on the loose. This
has attracted the attention of the GCPD and the billionaire-turned-vigilante Bruce Wayne. With tensions rising high,
the Batman must become the true detective and wade his way through the dark underbelly of Gotham to find the killer. But can he do it?
